Facility:

PhysioPDX shares space with Every Body Physical Therapy, another independent sole practitioner private practice. Our clinics co-exist in a spacious 4-treatment room clinic, with a large open gym, ADA restroom and accessibility throughout, and the basic equipment necessary for all treatment we provide. The clinic is located in the garden level suites, which are just below the level of the sidewalk, on the side of the building facing Flanders St. There are staircases on either side of the sub-sidewalk level leading down to the clinic. If you are seeing signs for EBPT, you are heading in the right direction.


PARKING is free in the Flanders Professional Building lot, located mid block between 22nd and 23rd ave, with entrances on both Everett St and Flanders St. There is also ample street parking throughout the neighborhood.
