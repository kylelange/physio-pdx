Facility:

PhysioPDX shares space with Every Body Physical Therapy. Our clinics co-exist in a spacious 4-treatment room clinic, with a large open gym, ADA restroom and accessibility throughout. The clinic is located on the side of the building facing Flanders St. There are staircases on either side leading down to the clinic. If you are seeing signs for EBPT, you are heading in the right direction.

PARKING is free in the Flanders Professional Building lot, located mid block between 22nd and 23rd ave, with entrances on both Everett St and Flanders St. There is also ample street parking throughout the neighborhood.
